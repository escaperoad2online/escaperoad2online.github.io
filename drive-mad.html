About Drive Mad

Drive Mad is an exciting car game where you navigate a challenging track packed with obstacles, aiming to reach the finish line without crashing. The key is to carefully control your speed to prevent your car from flipping over, which is trickier than it seems due to the thrilling stunts and creative obstacles. Can you conquer every level and prove your driving skills in Drive Mad?

How to Play Drive Mad

  • Steer Forward: Use W, D, X, Up Arrow, Right Arrow, or Mouse Click.
  • Steer Backward: Use S, A, Z, Down Arrow, or Left Arrow.
